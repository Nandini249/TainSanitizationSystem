Mission

"Our Missions: Keeping It Clean! The Automated Sanitization Revolution!"

Our mission is to revolutionize public transportation hygiene by automating the sanitization of train compartments using cutting-edge technology. We aim to create a safer and healthier travel environment for passengers by implementing an efficient, automated cleaning system that utilizes UV-C light, disinfectant sprayers, and HEPA filtration. By integrating sensors and IoT technology, we can ensure that the system operates only when needed, reducing energy consumption while maintaining a high standard of cleanliness. Our solution minimizes human intervention, providing consistent and thorough sanitization in every compartment.
